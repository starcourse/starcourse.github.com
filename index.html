about

Originated in 1892, Star Course is a live-concert organization run completely by students dedicated to the promotion and production of entertainment events for both the students of the University of Illinois and the Champaign-Urbana community. These events often include bands of various genres, but Star Course has also presented orchestras, comedy acts, and a variety of talented individuals.
